"Wisdom is not a product of schooling but of the lifelong attempt to acquire it" -Albert Einstein

I'm a Bionanotechnology Engineer graduated from Universidad Iberoamericana León and just finished my Master's Degree in Integrative Biology from LANGEBIO-CINVESTAV. During my master's degree I worked mainly in Bioinformatics using different programming languages like Python, Unix and R for data analysis. Being a bioinformatician reminded me how much I love programming from my high school and college years. Software Engineering was one of my desired careers but, at the time I entered college, I was more curious for biology and chemistry.

JS caught my attention because of the versatility to create programs, from web to mobile applications. I'm interested in learning JS technologies like React, React Native, Angular and Node.js to explore the potential that JS has to offer. I'm currently looking for a software engineer internship, so I can keep honing my skills and learning from experienced engineers.
